Quality & Sourcing Assurance

We eliminate the "Desk Trading" risk entirely. Our Expert Export Manager and ground team are physically present at processing units during production and loading. We provide NABL-accredited lab reports (SGS / Intertek / TUV) for every batch, covering microbial limits, moisture content, and pesticide residues — documentation provided before the shipment leaves India.

Logistics & Trade Operations

We primarily export from Mundra Port (Gujarat) and JNPT (Nhava Sheva, Mumbai) — India's two most efficient and well-connected gateways for global trade. These ports provide faster transit times to the Gulf (3–5 days), Europe (15–20 days), and the Americas (20–25 days), with excellent FCL and LCL options.

Yes. We offer full customized packaging solutions including 20kg / 25kg Kraft paper bags, HDPE woven bags, and vacuum-sealed inner liners. We also offer branded retail-ready packaging with your brand name and logo. Minimum order quantities apply for branded packaging.

For standard products, our lead time is 7 to 14 business days from the date of order confirmation and payment instrument receipt (LC / advance TT). Lead time varies based on volume, customization, and seasonal availability. Rush orders can be discussed with our Export Manager case-by-case.

Section 3

Payment & Financial Trust

Yes. At Glotrex, we believe in building long-term partnerships. While our standard protocols involve Confirmed Irrevocable L/C at Sight or T/T (30% Advance + 70% against BL Scan), we are open to discussing flexible payment structures based on order volume, frequency, and mutual trust developed over time.

We accept LCs from top-tier international banks including HSBC, Citibank, Standard Chartered, Deutsche Bank, Emirates NBD, and other globally reputed institutions. For specific regional banks, our finance team performs a standard audit to ensure a smooth and secure transaction. This typically takes 1–2 business days.
