ICC Standard Incoterms

Standardized Incoterms for
Seamless Global Delivery

We primarily operate under the following International Chamber of Commerce (ICC) standards. Customized delivery terms can be discussed based on specific port and country requirements.

FOB
Free On Board
Most Common

We ensure secure delivery of your goods to the Indian port and handle all export customs clearance. Risk transfers to the buyer once goods are on board the vessel.

Export customs & documentation — Glotrex
Loading at Indian port — Glotrex
Ocean freight — Buyer
Insurance — Buyer
Import customs — Buyer
CFR
Cost & Freight
Popular for GCC

Includes ocean freight to your designated destination port. Glotrex covers cost and freight; cargo insurance is arranged by the buyer.

Export customs & documentation — Glotrex
Ocean freight to destination — Glotrex
Loading at Indian port — Glotrex
Insurance — Buyer
Import customs — Buyer
CIF
Cost, Insurance & Freight
Full Coverage

Includes freight and comprehensive cargo insurance for 100% peace of mind. Glotrex handles everything up to the destination port of discharge.

Export customs & documentation — Glotrex
Ocean freight to destination — Glotrex
Cargo insurance — Glotrex
Unloading at destination — Buyer
Import customs — Buyer

Flexibility Note: Specific Incoterms can be discussed and finalized based on the volume, frequency, and destination of the shipment. Our Expert Export Manager will recommend the most cost-effective and secure option for your trade lane.

Financial Security & Trust

Secure, Tailored &
Transparent Payment Protocols

To ensure the highest level of financial security, we utilize established international banking channels. We support bank-guaranteed payment methods to protect the interests of both parties.

🏦
Method 1 — Most Secure

Confirmed Irrevocable
Letter of Credit (L/C) at Sight

The most secure bank-guaranteed method recognized globally — providing full protection for both the buyer and the exporter. Accepted from all top-tier international banks.

1
Buyer's bank issues a Confirmed Irrevocable L/C to Glotrex's bank
2
Glotrex processes and ships the order as per LC terms
3
Shipping documents presented to bank; payment released at sight
4
Buyer receives documents to clear customs at destination port
💸
Method 2 — Flexible

Telegraphic Transfer (T/T)
Advance & Against BL

Secure wire transfer payment — advance deposit confirms the order; remaining balance paid against scanned shipping documents (BL Copy) before document release.

1
30% advance T/T payment to confirm order & begin production
2
Glotrex completes production, QA, and port loading
3
Scanned BL copy shared; buyer remits remaining 70%
4
Original documents couriered upon balance payment confirmation
