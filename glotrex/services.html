5-Phase Process:
Inquiry to Port Loading

A systematic, Standard Operating Procedure followed on every single order — no exceptions, no shortcuts.

⚙ Phase 1

Requirement Audit & Technical Consultation

We don't just take orders — we analyze. Whether it's a specific mesh size for onion powder, curcumin levels for turmeric, or GSM for industrial textiles, our Expert Export Manager ensures the sourcing perfectly matches your industrial application and destination-country compliance.

🌾 Phase 2

Ethical Sourcing & Physical Sampling

We source exclusively from verified processing units registered with APEDA. Before main bulk production begins, we provide physical samples to you for independent lab validation (SGS / Intertek) — ensuring zero discrepancies between sample and shipment.

APEDA-registered units only Pre-production physical sample 100% Indian origin assured
🏭 Phase 3

Ground-Zero Supervision — Production & Packing

This is the expert difference. Our team is physically present at the processing unit during production. We supervise grading, sorting, moisture-control processes, and final packing to prevent any quality deviation. You receive live photos and videos from the production floor.

Physical presence at factory Live photo / video updates Grading & sorting oversight Moisture & purity checks

4
🔬 Phase 4

Multi-Stage Quality Assurance

Every batch undergoes rigorous testing at NABL-accredited laboratories. We verify microbial limits (Salmonella, E. Coli, Yeast/Mould), pesticide residues, and chemical properties based on the destination country's specific regulations — EU, US FDA, GCC, or others.

NABL-accredited lab reports SGS / Intertek / TUV EU & US FDA compliance Pesticide & microbial testing
🚢 Phase 5

Secure Palletization & Port Loading

We supervise container stuffing at Mundra or JNPT port. High-quality palletization, moisture-absorbent silica gels, and secure lashing are standard protocols. Pre-shipment inspection by your nominated agency (SGS, Bureau Veritas, etc.) is always welcomed and encouraged.

Mundra / JNPT port loading Silica gel & moisture control Pre-shipment inspection welcome Secure container stuffing
